## Features Overview

### Order Sync
Automatically create Zoho Books invoices when WooCommerce orders are placed. Configure which order statuses trigger sync and whether invoices are created as drafts or submitted immediately.

### Customer Sync
Automatically create or match Zoho Books contacts based on WooCommerce customer data. Map custom fields between systems.

### Product Mapping
Link WooCommerce products to Zoho Books items. Create new items or map to existing ones.

### Payment Sync
Record payments in Zoho Books when orders are completed. Map payment gateways to Zoho payment modes and bank accounts.

### Reconciliation
Compare WooCommerce orders with Zoho Books invoices to identify discrepancies, missing records, or sync failures.
